Why Koren Academy

A calmer, deeper way to learn Maths and Physics.

Koren Academy helps students truly understand Maths and Physics, not just memorise formulas. Students are guided to ask questions, test ideas, explain their reasoning and discover the logic behind each concept.

See how lessons work
1

Concepts before shortcuts

We slow down at the right moments so students understand why a method works before they use it repeatedly.

2

Active thinking in small groups

Students are encouraged to ask questions, think aloud and learn through guided discussion.

3

Exam style practice with meaning

Practice is linked back to the underlying idea, helping students approach unfamiliar questions more calmly.

“I get the formula, but I do not know why it works.”

That is exactly the gap we teach into. Students learn where ideas come from, not just what to write in an exam.

“I can do examples, but harder questions throw me.”

Lessons build from clear examples to exam style questions, so students practise recognising what a question is really asking.

Lesson structure

A clear rhythm, built around active thinking.

Each lesson runs for 2 hours and 15 minutes. Students revise, review mistakes, learn theory, work through examples and practise exam style questions.

1

Short quiz

The first 15 minutes revisit previous content and homework.

2

Quiz review

Mistakes are discussed early so weak areas do not stay hidden.

3

Theory and derivation

Students learn where ideas come from, not just what to write.

4

Basic examples

Core techniques are modelled clearly before harder questions.

5

Exam style questions

Students practise applying ideas to questions that require reasoning.

6

Booklets and homework

Students receive resources and homework to continue practising after class.
